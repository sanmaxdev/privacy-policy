EasyAds Downloader respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, use, store, or share any personal information or data from users of the EasyAds Downloader Chrome extension. The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not transmit any data to external servers or third parties.

Permissions

The extension requires certain permissions to function correctly:

  • activeTab: Allows the extension to interact with the currently active tab to detect and access video content.
  • downloads: Enables the extension to save video files directly to the user's device.
  • Host permission: Allows the extension to interact with specific domains (e.g., facebook.com) to detect and download videos from the Facebook Ads Library.
